  • Equipment failure or malfunction

2. SAFETY RULES AND GUIDELINES

I agree to ensure that all participants follow these safety rules:

  • Maximum 8 children at one time (ages 3-12, max weight 150 lbs)

  • No rough play, climbing on walls, or flips
  • No adults over 150 lbs allowed inside
  • Exit immediately if weather conditions become unsafe
  • Adult supervision required at all times

I understand that I am responsible for providing adequate adult supervision during the entire rental period. The supervising adult must:

  • Contact the rental company immediately if any issues arise
